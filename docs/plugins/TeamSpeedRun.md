# Team Speed Run

Want to see what team can finish their tasks the fastest? Play TSR!

The crew is split into two two team, Red vs. Blue.  
Once a team finishes all their tasks, they win and the game ends.

The impostor(s) will be put on a team as well.  
Will you help your team by sabotaging the other team? Or maybe kill your team so they can travel the map faster?  
Don't kill too many people, or no one will win.

## Install

Follow the [SusSuite Plugin Install](/docs/plugins/Install.md) instructions.

Then use `/gamemode TeamSpeedRun` to play today.
